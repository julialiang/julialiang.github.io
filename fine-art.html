Fine Art

I've been drawing and painting ever since I can remember. I started with pencil and charcoal, and then incorporated colored pencil, pastels, watercolor, and oil paint.

My senior year of high school, I completed an AP Art portfolio, on which I received the highest score, 5. Two summers ago, I was selected as one of twenty students internationally for the Marie Walsh Sharpe Art Foundation's Summer Seminar, an intensive two-week art program. I have gained recognition for my distinctive artistic style, winning numerous first place ribbons and exceptional merit rosettes at the Arizona State Fair and in other national competitions.

Recent Work & Work in Progress

In college, I have been exploring art as a second major after taking introductory drawing and painting classes last year. My recent work is mostly in watercolor because I have found that it is one of the easiest mediums to travel with while I am away from my studio at home.

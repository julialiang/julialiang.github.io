I recently graduated from UC Berkeley, where I doubled majored in Computer Science and Practice of Art. I'm currently working as an Interaction Designer on Chrome. In the past, I've been fortunate enough to work at Apple, Google ATAP and Highway Twenty (now, Casual Astronaut). I'm always looking for opportunities to grow as a designer, so feel free to drop me a line.

Aside from design, I love ice skating and printmaking, and I wouldn't mind eating pickled vegetables for all my meals. I spend the majority of my weeknights and weekends serving on the college ministry team at my church.

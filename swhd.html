Southwest Human Development

Web Development

Southwest Human Development is the largest early childhood development nonprofit in Arizona.

At Highway Twenty, I had the pleasure of participating in the complete redesign and development of their new website.

I implemented consistency in content population and a clear typographic hierarchy across the 200 existing pages of this website. I also wrote many media queries, particularly for the custom elements on the home page to ensure graceful compatibility across mobile devices and tablets.

Please note that even though I made design decisions, I do not intend to take credit for entirety of the design or development of this website.
